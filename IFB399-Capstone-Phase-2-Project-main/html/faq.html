FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Who is this website for?

The ZALK website is designed for experienced muscicians to find local venues to book gigs as well as advertise themselves to muscical students looking to learn and improve their musical skills.

How do I book a lesson with a musical teacher?

Once you have registered and signed in to your account, navigate to the search page. Once you are on the search page, you will be able to saerch for a teacher/lesson which is suited for you. If you click on the lesson, fill out the booking request form to send a notification to the musician. From here on, the musician will be able to contact you via email.

How do I book a gig at a venue?

Navigate to the Venues page. Once you are on the Venues page, clicking on a venue that interests you will take you to that venue's booking website. From there, you are able to contact the venue to organise bookings.

How do I change my password and other settings?

Users are able to change any account settings by navigating to the Settings page. From there, users can update their password.

How do I talk to my student/teacher?

Once a booking request has been made, it is up to the musician to contact the student via email.
